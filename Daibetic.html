Endocrine, Diabetes and Hypertension specialist care:

Introduction to Endocrine, Diabetes and Hypertension specialist care:

Endocrine, Diabetes, and Hypertension specialist care focuses on managing hormone imbalances, blood sugar levels, and high blood pressure. Endocrinologists treat hormone-related issues, while diabetes care involves controlling blood sugar to prevent complications. Hypertension treatment aims to reduce cardiovascular risks. Specialists offer tailored treatment plans, advanced diagnostics, and ongoing care for better health outcomes.

    Endocrine

    Diagnosis and management of endocrine disorders (thyroid, adrenal, pituitary diseases).

  • Evaluation and treatment of hormone imbalances (testosterone, estrogen, growth hormone).
  • Osteoporosis and metabolic bone disease management.
  • Polycystic Ovary Syndrome (PCOS) management.
  • Treatment of calcium and parathyroid-related disorders (hypercalcemia, hypocalcemia).

    Diabetes Services

    Diagnosis and management of Type 1, Type 2, and gestational diabetes.

  • Blood glucose monitoring and HbA1C testing.
  • Insulin therapy initiation and adjustment.
  • Diabetes education and lifestyle counseling.
  • Management of diabetes complications (neuropathy, retinopathy, nephropathy).

    Hypertension Services

    Diagnosis and treatment of primary and secondary hypertension.

  • Blood pressure monitoring and management plans.
  • Evaluation of hypertension-related complications (cardiovascular or kidney disease).
  • Lifestyle modification and dietary counseling
